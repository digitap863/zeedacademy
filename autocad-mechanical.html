AUTOCAD MECHANICAL TRAINING AND CERTIFICATION

AutoCAD® Mechanical is the design and drafting software from Autodesk with focus on mechanical design process. The program provides a meticulous, CAD drafting platform for manufacturing, with highly resourceful tools to streamline documentation and ensures accuracy. Bringing together all the functionalities of fundamental AutoCAD software and the tools for mechanical designing, the program gives a competitive edge to professionals, providing increased productivity and scope for innovation. Accelerating the process of Engineering Drawing, AutoCAD® Mechanical integrates highly adaptive and diverse features like International Drafting standards support, specialized tools to add, delete, and edit dimensions; highly proficient techniques for documentation drawing etc; It helps to creates detail views of 3D models and facilitates instant updates that automatically communicate changes to the design and manufacturing teams.

Features At A Glance
  • ♦ More than 7,00,000 Standard Parts and Features
  • ♦ Extended Draw Toolbar for Manufacturing
  • ♦ Powerful and Smart Dimensions
  • ♦ Reusable Detailing Tools
  • ♦ Associative Balloons and Bill of Materials (BOMs)
  • ♦ Layer Management
  • ♦ Machinery Generators and Calculators
  • ♦ Data Exchange between CAD Systems
Industry

Mechanical CAD is prominently used in various engineering manufacturing fields like Industrial equipment, Automotive, Transportation, Oil & Gas industries.

Pre-Requisites
