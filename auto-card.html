AUTOCAD ESSENTIALS TRAINING AND CERTIFICATION

This is a foundation level course which is intended to familiarize beginners with AutoCAD /AutoCAD LT user interfaces and provide a comprehensive knowledge in the basics of Computer Aided Design.
Essentials 2015 helps a novice in CAD to get versatile with the drawing and designing process in 2D. AutoCAD training and certification course enables one to communicate their ideas precisely through technical drawings. It will enable one to convert the blueprint concepts of AutoCAD drawings to accurate 2D construction designs!

FRONT OFFICE OPERATIONS
  • ♦ Basic drawing, editing and viewing
  • ♦ Creating and handling objects in layers
  • ♦ Splines and polylines
  • ♦ Arranging objects with groups and blocks
  • ♦ Real-world exercises in AutoCAD and AutoCAD LT
  • ♦ AutoCAD tasks, tools, and core functionality in the context of designing a house
  • ♦ Blocks and cross-references
  • ♦ Dimensioning, Constraints and layouts
  • ♦ Printing, editing and importing data
  • ♦ 3D modeling concepts
  • ♦ Presenting the design
ELIGIBLE CANDIDATES:
  • ♦ AutoCAD Beginners
  • ♦ Civil/Mechanical Engineering Students/professionals
  • ♦ Product Designers & Developers
  • ♦ Architects, Interior Designers
  • ♦ Industrial Designers
  • ♦ Landscape Architects
SYLLABUS
  • ♦ The AutoCAD Interface
  • ♦ Creating Basic Drawings
  • ♦ Accurate object Creation
  • ♦ Object Viewing
  • ♦ Manipulating Objects
  • ♦ Altering Objects
  • ♦ Drawing Organisation
  • ♦ Working with Layouts
  • ♦ Annotating the Drawing
  • ♦ Dimensioning
  • ♦ Hatching Objects
  • ♦ Working with Reusable Content
  • ♦ Plotting Your Drawings
  • ♦ Creating Drawing Templates

No prior experience in CAD required.

DURATION: 80 HRS
