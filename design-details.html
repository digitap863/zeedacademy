INTERIOR DESIGNING

Contemporary interior design involves much more than deciding which pillows and drapes tie the room together — although those selections still matter. At Frankel Building Group, we believe interior design is both science and art! It has to do with how our brains and bodies react to the space around us. And it’s the job of an interior designer to shape those feelings or sensations in a positive direction that makes someone want to spend time in a room.

FASHION DESIGNING

Fashion designers initiate and oversee the entire creative process that goes into creating articles of clothing-from conception to finished garment. Basically, this process begins when designers come up with a concept that they turn into a sketch. Next, they determine specifics such as the garment’s dimensions, what color it should be, what fabric should be used and create a pattern based on their conclusions

WEB DESIGNING

Web design refers to the design of websites that are displayed on the internet. It usually refers to the user experience aspects of website development rather than software development. Web design used to be focused on designing websites for desktop browsers; however, since the mid-2010s, design for mobile and tablet browsers has become ever-increasingly important.
